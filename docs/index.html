How It Works

1

Download & Install

Download the application for your platform (Windows/macOS/Linux). Extract and run - no complex installation needed.

2

Watch It Come Alive

The terrarium appears at the bottom of your screen. Plants start growing, creatures begin moving, and your ecosystem takes shape.

3

Click to feed hungry creatures or water thirsty plants. Hover to make plants dance. Use keyboard shortcuts to spawn new entities.

4

Let It Live

Your ecosystem runs autonomously. Check in occasionally, or just let it exist peacefully in the background. It saves automatically.

  4. Grant necessary permissions for transparency effects
  5. Enjoy your living desktop ecosystem!
