## üéÆ Game Modes

### 1. Typical Terrarium (Forest, Desert, Aquatic)
**Best for**: Relaxation, Background visuals.
- **What it is**: A self-sustaining ecosystem that lives on your desktop.
- **How to play**: 
    - Use the **transparent window** to let it run while you work.
    - Click to feed creatures or water plants.
    - Watch the food chain evolve (Plants -> Herbivores -> Carnivores).
    - **Keys**: `F` to follow a creature, `Space` to pause.

### 2. God Simulator üåç
**Best for**: Experimentation, Chaos.
- **What it is**: A world of warring factions where you play god.
- **How to play**:
    - Choose a theme (Forest, Desert, Aquatic).
    - Use **Keys 1-5** to cast powers:
        - `1` Spawn Life
        - `2` Smite (Lightning)
        - `3` Mutate
        - `4` Heal
        - `5` Freeze
    - Watch factions expand, fight, and build lore.

- **How to play**:
    - Left Click to draw elements (Sand, Water, Fire, etc.).
    - Right Click to erase.
    - Experiment with reactions (Water + Fire = Steam!).

### 4. Civilization Builder üèõÔ∏è
**Best for**: Strategy fans.

### 5. 2048 Widget
A classic sliding tile puzzle.
- **Goal**: Combine tiles to reach the number 2048!
- **Controls**: Arrow Keys to slide tiles. `R` to restart.

### 6. Tetris Widget
The legendary block-stacking game.
- **Goal**: Complete lines to clear them and score points.
- **Controls**:
  - `Left/Right`: Move Piece
  - `Up`: Rotate
  - `Down`: Soft Drop
  - `Space`: Hard Drop
  - `P`: Pause

### 7. T-Rex Runner
A dinosaur infinite runner.
- **Goal**: Jump over cacti and survive as long as possible.
- **Controls**: `Space` or `Up` to Jump. `Down` to Duck.

### 8. Arcade Widget (Snake)
A classic Snake game.
- **Controls**: Arrow Keys to move. `R` to restart.

### 9. Web Widget
A borderless browser window.
- **Goal**: Loads `play2048.co` by default but can load any URL.

### 10. Desktop Pet
A small virtual companion that walks around your screen.
- **Controls**: Click and drag to move it. Moves towards mouse.

### 11. Pacman Widget
The arcade legend.
- **Goal**: Eat all dots. Avoid ghosts.
- **Controls**: Arrow Keys.

## ‚ùì Troubleshooting & Requirements

### ‚ö†Ô∏è "Python is missing" Error
Modes 2, 3, and 4 require **Python 3.8+** to run. 
1. Download Python from [python.org](https://www.python.org/downloads/).
2. **Important**: Check the box **"Add Python to PATH"** during installation.
3. Restart the application.

### Window Not Transparent?
- Ensure Windows "Aero" or transparency effects are enabled in your personalized settings.

### Performance Issues?
- Press `F1` to toggle the status panel and check FPS.
- If it's slow, try restarting to clear accumulated entities.
